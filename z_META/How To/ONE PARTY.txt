So you are wondering how you make your nation a "one-party nation"?

Well, you have come to the right guide!

This is more for coding, so if you see this and are wondering how you can make your nation one-party in the game.. sorry :/

IF YOU ARE CODING IT IN:

Add this to your events/history file/whatever:
set_country_flag = one_party_nation

and to remove it:
clr_country_flag = one-party
